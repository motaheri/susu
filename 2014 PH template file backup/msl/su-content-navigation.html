You can login as a Swansea University Student, a Guest or as Students' Union Staff.

As a University Student your user name will be your student number. Example: 765432
Your default password is derived from your date of birth unless you have changed it. Please take a moment to read more about your password details here.

Society or Sports club Presidents and Captains please use this website guide.

You should create a Guest account if you want to join societies, sports or buy event tickets but you are Not a current Swansea University student or a Students' Union Staff member. Swansea University Staff and previous students should also create a new guest account.

New Union staff members should contact the web development team to be given an email address then create a Staff Account here.

As a Swansea University Student you can reset your password here, if needed.

As a Guest or a Union Staff member you can reset your password here, if needed.
